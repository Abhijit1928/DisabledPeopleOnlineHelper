Reality:-


Education is very important for every person regardless of his/her age, gender, economic status, as well as physical ability or disability….
students with physical disabilities experience personal limitations in school/College environments that affect their social, psychological and academic spheres that may likely affect their academic performances at school/Colleges…..
As we can see now days disable people face many problem while giving examination…
- They face problem while studying.
- They get tense sometimes while thinking about examination…
- They face difficulty while finding writer who help them in exam...

About this Application:-


- This Platform help disable children to solve their problems regarding exam
- This Platform will help disable students to choose their writer or reader.
- They can chose them according to their qualification.
- Due to this Platform we can make easier for disable student to proceed their education .
- System will find the helper person within the time..
- Through this platform the disable person will share their feedback and encourage to other people to help disable person..
